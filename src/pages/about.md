I'm Agata Migalska. By day I work in GenAI systems. By personal obsession, I chase structure in the physical world — GNSS, 3D reconstruction, drones, graphs.

This blog lives somewhere between those two worlds. It's rooted in things I explored during my PhD in Computer Science and am now picking back up as a personal project: how we position things in space, how we rebuild geometry from sensors, how we model what we see. And occasionally, how graphs turn up in places you wouldn't expect — like climbing routes, or the path your eyes take across an image.

Each series follows one of these threads. The underlying question is always the same: *what is the structure here, and what does it tell us?*

I publish every few weeks. You can reach me at [agata@migalska.com](mailto:agata@migalska.com) or grab the [RSS feed](/rss.xml).
